# Gameplay Loop

Scale Navigator is a tool for exploring, capturing, and sharing harmony.

```text
EXPLORE
• Navigate neighboring scales
• Explore chords within each scale
• Discover interesting harmonic states

↓

CAPTURE
• Save harmonic states
• Arrange them into progressions
• Create custom scales/chords

↓

SHARE
• Play on real instruments (tablature)
• Drive DAWs/plugins (MIDI)
• Perform together (Ensemble Jammer)

↓

Repeat
```

## Explore

- **Navigator** — move between neighboring scales
- **Scale Network** — global map of harmonic space
- **Chord Network** — navigate chords within the current scale
- **Voice-leading controls** — influence automatic chord suggestions
- **Pivot chords** — suggest neighboring scales (a chord shared by two scales = a doorway between them)

## Capture

- Save discovered harmonic states
- Create custom harmonic states
- Arrange progressions visually
- Score records exploration history

## Share

- Tablature
- MIDI / plugin ecosystem
- Ensemble Jammer multiplayer

## Ensemble Jammer

Ensemble Jammer is the multiplayer performance client. The Dashboard explores and broadcasts harmony; Ensemble Jammer follows that harmonic state and constrains every instrument to it, allowing groups to improvise together without wrong notes.

---

Everything in Explore, Capture, and Share can be experienced solo today. The game extends this loop rather than replacing it.

---

## The MMODAW Loop

The game wraps the solo loop in three shared spaces. Explore/Capture/Share still happen — now they happen with other people, across a workbench, a rehearsal room, and a venue.

```text
WORKBENCH
• Explore harmony and capture what you like
• Arrange it into a progression / song
• Craft together in a multiplayer DAW

↓

REHEARSAL (VR)
• Bring friends into the song
• Orchestrate: assign parts, swap instruments
• Practice difficult transitions
• Decide who carries melody, who improvises
• Play it through together, Guitar Hero style

↓

VENUE (VR / AR)
• Perform live for an audience of avatars
• The performer drives harmony; the band improvises around it

↓

ARTIFACT
• The session leaves the game: song, MIDI, Ableton project, stems, recording

↓

Repeat
```

- **Workbench** — where the existing Explore → Capture → Share loop becomes collaborative songwriting.
- **Rehearsal** — the new phase most music games skip: a collaborative, almost management-style step where a solo arrangement becomes an ensemble.
- **Venue** — the payoff: a live performance others can attend, watch, and walk away with a copy of.

Every trip through the loop is meant to leave players with something they can take into the rest of their musical life.
